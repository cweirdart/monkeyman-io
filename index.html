About

Just spreading good music for your soul.

MonkeyMan is an Oakland-based DJ and selector rooted in funk, disco, underground house, and classics. From dusty vinyl crates to packed dance floors, it's always about the groove.

A regular on the Bay Area circuit, MonkeyMan has rocked venues from The Great Northern and Monarch to Ruby Room and The Valencia Room. Featured on Fault Radio San Francisco and a guest on La Casa Discoteca Radio Show, he's shared stages with acts like Young Franco, Kenny Summit, Jayvi Velasco, and Lady Ryan.
